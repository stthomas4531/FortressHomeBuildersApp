About Us

Christopher Fisher, born and raised in Coram, MT has a beautifully creative mind. His craft and workmanship comes through from concept to completion, his attention to detail and artistic eye bring every masterpiece to life.

Christopher is a wonderful father and husband, to Keshia and his five beautiful children, Noeley(8), Harper(7), Kingston(5), Deacon(3), & Clarke(18 months). Both being born and raised in the Flathead Valley, building family and business here is important to them.

Owning and operating Fisher Properties - Vacation Rental properties in Coram. As well as Glacier Highline one of Glacier Park's newest premier outdoor activities. A high ropes aerial adventure park also located in Coram. A board member in his local church, Flood in Columbia Falls. He's after God's heart and purpose for his life, seeking to better our community one project at a time.
