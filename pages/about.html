Owning and operating Fisher Properties - Vacation Rental properties in Coram. As well as Glacier Highline one of Glacier Park's newest premier outdoor activities. A high ropes aerial adventure park also located in Coram. A board member in his local church, Flood in Columbia Falls. He's after God's heart and purpose for his life, seeking to better our community one project at a time.
